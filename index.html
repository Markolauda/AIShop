In the serene village of Boljevci, near Belgrade, two friends, steeped in a shared passion for healthy living and the rich bounty of the land, embarked on a flavorful journey. Together, they founded Uberry, a tribute to the lush fields surrounding the village. In the quietude of Boljevci, their friendship blossomed into a commitment to crafting jams that embraced the wholesome goodness of locally grown berries. Uberry stands as a testament to the enduring bond of friendship and the vibrant flavors that emerged from the heart of the land.
